The beach of Pointe Vénus is one of the most famous of the French Polynesian island. This beach of the Matavai bay is clean, its sand is of a unique black color, and swimming is supervised. The Pointe de Vénus is located north of Papeete, after the commune of Arue.
The beach of Pointe Vénus offers all the amenities. Parking, toilets, picnic tables are available for visitors. Stores are also located along the nearby lighthouse. Come and admire the breathtaking view of the island of Moorea from Pointe Vénus beach.
