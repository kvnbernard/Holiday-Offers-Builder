Tautira beach is located on the north coast of the peninsula, in Tautira Bay. It can be reached by a path near the Vaitepiha River or near the village.
The beach of Tautira offers an incredible view of the Vaitepiha valley. The heights of these valleys are of extraordinary beauty. Moreover, it is a marvelous beach whose black sand is of an incomparable fineness.
The clear and calm water of the beach allows a bathing in all quietness. In addition, Tautira beach has showers, tables and a petanque court. Games have also been installed for children.
