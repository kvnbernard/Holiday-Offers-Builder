Do you dream of approaching a dolphin in Tahiti? Come and admire these aquatic players twirling above the waters. Come and meet these magnificent and friendly mammals who only want to amaze you.
The luckiest among you will have the joy of swimming alongside them and meeting these dolphins in their natural environment, but only if they allow you to.
This encounter will simply be a unique and wonderful experience that you will never forget!
NB: Dolphin trips are not available during the whale season (August to October).
